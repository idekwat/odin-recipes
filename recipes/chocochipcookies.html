Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

This Chocolate Chip Cookie recipe is obviously not the best recipe there is but I personally think it's good enough to get the job done especially since it's easy to make and doesn't taste bad.

Ingredients

  • Butter 100g
  • Flour 100g
  • White Sugar 75g
  • Brown Sugar 75g
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 Tsp Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Melt your butter in a pan or in the microwave, you can brown it if you want to. Once melted, trasnfer to a large bowl and let it cool for a bit
  2. While the butter is cooling, in a separate bowl, combine flour, a pinch of salt, baking powder, and baking soda. Once combined, whisk thoroughly.
  3. Once the butter isn't hot to the touch, add both sugars and whisk. After a few seconds, add the egg and whisk until the sugar is fully dissolved, then add a splash of vanilla.
  4. Slowly add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients then combine until the dough is formed then add as much chocolate chips as you want and mix.
  5. Let the dough rest for an hour or overnight
  6. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes at an oven pre-heated at 200c.
