Milkshake

image from Allrecipes.com

Description

Milkshakes are a very simple and delicious way to cool your day. Easily customizable according to your taste.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Ice Cream(Vanilla for most, but it depends on what flavor you are doing)
  • Your choice of ingredients(Peanut butter, Vanilla Essence, Coffee, Fruits, the choices are endless)
  • Toppings optional

Steps

  1. In a blender add 1 cup of milk and 2 cups of your chosen Ice Cream. The amount can be adjusted, add more ice cream if you want a thicker milkshake and add more milk if you want it runnier.
  2. Blend the ice cream and milk until they start to mix together then add your other ingredients, blend until smooth.
  3. Serve in a tall glass and add your chosen garnish and serve.
